About

I am a lecturer at Posts and Telecommunications Institute of Technology. My research interests include Combinatorics Coding and Information Theory Bioinformatics.

Welcome! I recently joined the AI Division at PTIT as a Lecturer. My academic journey began in pure Mathematics, where I focused on Combinatorics and Coding Theory during my PhD. I later spent time as a Postdoc exploring the world of Bioinformatics. As a lifelong learner, I am currently deepening my knowledge of AI and its biological applications. This year, I look forward to sharing my passion for logic and structure through my Discrete Mathematics course and other upcoming subjects.
